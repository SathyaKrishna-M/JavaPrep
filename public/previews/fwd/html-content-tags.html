Common HTML Content Tags

<h1> to <h6>

Heading 1 (Main Title)

Heading 2 (Section Title)

Heading 3 (Subsection)

<p> and <strong>

This is a standard paragraph. It adds spacing before and after the text block.

You can make text bold (strong) or italic (em) for emphasis.

<ul> and <ol>

Unordered List:

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Cherries

Ordered List:

  1. Step one
  2. Step two
  3. Step three
<a> (Anchor/Link)

Links connect the web together. Go to Google (opens in new tab).

<img>

Images bring color to the page:

Note: Always include an `alt` text for accessibility!
